Congratulations on taking an exciting step by enrolling in our one-month Summer Camp program! We’re thrilled to welcome you and your child into the Moonpreneur community. Over the next few weeks, your child will dive into an unforgettable experience that goes beyond just learning robotics or coding. This camp is about sparking creativity, boosting confidence, and developing a mindset geared for success in tomorrow’s world. We can’t wait for you to explore the richness of our ecosystem—a vibrant space crafted to ignite innovation, foster future-ready skills, and help your child unlock their fullest potential. Get ready for an amazing summer adventure!

The future of work requires technical skills, soft skills, and resilience. Moonpreneur prepares students through a product-building lifecycle, equipping them with skills and verifiable profiles for college admissions and future employers. Research shows early missed opportunities are hard to recover, and excessive screen time in children can lead to behavioral issues, delayed milestones, and problems like sleep issues, obesity, depression, and anxiety. Redirecting this time to skill-building fosters healthier habits and a stronger future.
